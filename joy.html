Why LizOn Education Was Created

In Bangladesh, I saw thousands of students with big dreams getting lost in a maze of generic coaching centers. Many were being misled by flashy advertisements rather than real results.

Students often struggle with a fundamental confusion: they treat IELTS or PTE as a traditional academic subject rather than a test of strategy. They spend months in classrooms without ever understanding why they are failing to hit their target scores.

I realized that students don't fail because their English is inherently "bad." They fail because of a lack of structure and the absence of a personalized roadmap.
