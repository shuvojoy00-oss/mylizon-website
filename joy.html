Why I Created LizOn Education

After completing my Bachelor’s in Australia and my Masters in Bangkok, I returned to Bangladesh with a clear observation: our students are incredibly talented, but they are being misled by a system built on false promises and generic coaching.

Every day, I see students stuck in a loop of confusion. They move from one coaching center to another, hearing the same "tips and tricks" that don't work in real exams. They are burdened with the fear of visas and the complexity of financial documentation, often guided by agencies that prioritize profit over the student's career.

Realizing that students don’t fail because of their English—they fail because of a lack of structure, the wrong preparation system, and a complete absence of proper guidance—I decided to act.

I created LizOn Education not to be just another coaching center, but to be a SYSTEM. A system that focuses on logic, strategy, and a clear roadmap to global opportunities.
