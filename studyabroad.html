Australia

Study in Australia

Why students choose Australia

  • Globally recognised degrees
  • Strong part-time work culture
  • Post-study work opportunities (not guaranteed PR)
  • Safe, multicultural, English-speaking environment

Probable intakes

  • February — main intake
  • July — second major intake
  • November — limited courses

IELTS / PTE requirement

  • IELTS minimum: 6.0 | Recommended: 6.5+
  • PTE minimum: 50–58 | Recommended: 58–65+

Documents needed

  • Academic transcripts
  • IELTS or PTE score report
  • Passport
  • Financial documents (profile-based)
  • GS / SOP

Work & job reality

  • Common student jobs: hospitality, retail, warehouses
  • City matters: Sydney & Melbourne vs regional areas
  • English skill and effort matter

After study opportunities

Temporary graduate visas and skill-based pathways depend on demand, policy, and timing.

Common mistakes students make

  • Applying without IELTS/PTE score ready
  • Choosing city without understanding cost
  • Rushing due to agent pressure
