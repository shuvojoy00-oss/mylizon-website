IELTS 2026 Update: Paper-Based Writing is Still Available!

Important News for Bangladeshi Students:

While the British Council and IDP have shifted toward Computer-Delivered IELTS as the primary format in 2026, you can still choose to take your Writing Module on Paper at selected centers.

How the Hybrid Test Works

For those who find typing difficult or prefer the feel of a pen, the new hybrid option allows you to customize your experience:

  • Listening & Reading: Completed on a computer screen.
  • Writing: Handwritten on a physical answer sheet.
  • Speaking: Face-to-face interview with a human examiner.
