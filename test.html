Frequently Asked Questions

What is PTE?

PTE (Pearson Test of English) is an English proficiency test for students who want to study abroad. এটি IELTS এর মতো কিন্তু automated এবং faster result দেয়।

Who can join this course?

Any Bangladeshi student planning to go abroad for higher studies can join. No advanced English level needed — আমরা basics থেকে শুরু করি।

Can I join from anywhere in Bangladesh?

Yes! Classes are conducted live online via Zoom, so তুমি দেশের যেকোনো জায়গা থেকে join করতে পারো।
