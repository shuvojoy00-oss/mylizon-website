Study Abroad from Bangladesh

This guide is designed for Bangladeshi students who want to study abroad with clarity. It explains country selection, cost ranges, scholarships, and job opportunities in a realistic way.

Where to Study

  • Bachelor’s Degree – Ideal after HSC or A Levels
  • Master’s Degree – For graduates aiming career growth
  • By Profession – Business, IT, Engineering, Health, Arts

Cost Range (Minimum to Maximum)

Australia

Tuition + Living: 28 – 45 BDT Lac / year

United Kingdom

Tuition + Living: 22 – 35 BDT Lac / year

Canada

Tuition + Living: 20 – 32 BDT Lac / year

Ireland

Tuition + Living: 18 – 28 BDT Lac / year

Hungary

Tuition + Living: 10 – 16 BDT Lac / year

Netherlands / Denmark / Sweden

Tuition + Living: 20 – 30 BDT Lac / year

Scholarship Criteria

  • Academic results (GPA / CGPA)
  • English proficiency (IELTS / PTE)
  • Strong SOP and academic consistency
  • Relevant background for the chosen subject

What students can do now:

  • Improve English score
  • Build academic profile
  • Gain relevant skills and certifications

Student Jobs & Skills

Common Part-Time Jobs

  • Retail assistant
  • Restaurant & café staff
  • Warehouse worker
  • Cleaner / support staff

Skills That Help

  • Communication & customer handling
  • Basic computer & POS usage
  • Time management
  • Adaptability in multicultural environment

Before Going Abroad

  • Learn basic work etiquette
  • Improve spoken English
  • Understand local job culture
