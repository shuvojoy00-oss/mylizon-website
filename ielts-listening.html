Why IELTS Listening feels hard (even after practice)

IELTS doesn’t test “understanding”. It tests attention + prediction + accuracy.

  • Accent shock
  • Losing the next answer while thinking about the previous one
  • Spelling and number/date confusion
  • Corrections and paraphrase traps

What actually improves Listening scores

  • Predict before audio
  • Track keywords, not meaning
  • Recover instantly after a miss
  • Spelling discipline
