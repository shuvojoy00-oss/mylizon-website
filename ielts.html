IELTS ZERO — Full Details

For students who feel lost, confused, or scared to start IELTS.

What you will fix

  • Grammar confusion → clear foundation
  • Pronunciation/sound problems → clarity + confidence
  • Reading dependency on Bangla translation → start understanding directly
  • Speaking stuck (ideas + fluency) → controlled practice
  • Writing idea & structure blockage → guided structure

Who should take ZERO

  • You can understand English but can’t speak confidently
  • Grammar feels like “math” and always goes wrong
  • You don’t know where to start IELTS
  • You want a clean, step-by-step start
