Practice modules: Listening Reading Writing Speaking

Level 1

IELTS ZERO — English Starts Here

Jodi apnar problem hoy: grammar fear, sentence banate paren na, speaking/writing start hoy na — then foundation chara score change hoy na. Start here.

Duration: 1 Month Sessions: 12 Best for: First start
Admit Now See Price

Who should take ZERO?

  • Kothay theke shuru korbo bujhi na
  • Grammar e confusion & fear
  • Speaking e idea ashe na / confidence nai
  • Reading e translate chara bujhi na
  • Listening e subtitles chara answer dhorte pari na
  • Writing e structure + idea weak

What improves (foundation)

  • Grammar + sentence control
  • Speaking er basic confidence + pronunciation
  • Reading/Listening e understanding system
  • Writing e basic structure + idea practice

Target: confusion remove, ekta follow-kora routine set.

Level 2

IELTS HOPE — Your Reset Level

Base ase, kintu score atke thake. Reading slow, Listening miss hoy, Writing control nai, Speaking e smooth idea flow nai — HOPE fixes the blockers.

Duration: 1 Month Sessions: 16 Best for: Reset
Admit Now See Price

Who should take HOPE?

  • Basics ase, kintu “score convert” hoy na
  • Reading slow — time-e passage shesh hoy na
  • Listening e trap e dhora poren
  • Writing e structure thakleo ideas weak / coherence break
  • Speaking e opinion ase na / repeated answers

What improves (reset)

  • Reading speed + question-type strategy (no random practice)
  • Listening focus + prediction + mistake pattern control
  • Writing e Task 2 structure + idea development, Task 1 upgrading
  • Speaking e natural flow + opinion building + fluency control

Target: stuck loop break kore stable 6+ direction.

Level 3

IELTS STEP-UP — Go Beyond 6.0

Jara serious bhabe band 6 cross kore 6.5–7+ e move korte chan — STEP-UP e focus: accuracy, control, and real improvement.

Duration: 1 Month Sessions: 16 Best for: Band jump
Admit Now See Price

Who should take STEP-UP?

  • Band 5.5–6.0 e atke ase
  • Time-e performance bhenge jay (pressure e mistakes)
  • Writing e band descriptors meet hoy na (task response / coherence / grammar)
  • Speaking e Part 2 delivery weak / Part 3 depth ase na
  • Reading/Listening e accuracy stable na

What improves (band jump)

  • Accuracy + time management (exam-standard drills)
  • Writing e stronger argument + better coherence + fewer grammar slips
  • Speaking e vocabulary upgrading + natural linking + deeper ideas
  • Reading e passage control + smarter scanning + trap avoidance
  • Listening e section-wise strategy + speed adaptation

Target: 6.5–7+ direction with stable performance.

Level 4

IELTS DESPERATE — Fast. Final. Evaluation Focused

Exam near, or repeat koreo desired score pay nai — focus: weakness detect → fix → test → evaluate.

Duration: 1 Month Sessions: 28 Best for: Near exam
Admit Now See Price

Who should take DESPERATE?

  • Exam date near / last chance vibe
  • 2–3 bar diyeo score uthche na
  • Mock e score ashe, exam e panic e drop hoy
  • Writing e repeat mistakes (feedback chara same error)
  • Speaking e confidence break / consistency nai

What improves (evaluation loop)

  • Frequent test → correction → exact weak-point fixing
  • Speaking & Writing e feedback heavy cycle (real exam style)
  • Reading/Listening e mistake pattern diagnosis + timed practice
  • Confidence: panic kom, control beshi

Target: final push with maximum evaluation before exam.
